$$f_{n+1} = R(f_n, f_{n-1}) + ig(F_n) e_{n+1}$$

$$i\hbar \frac{\partial \psi}{\partial t} = \hat{H} \psi + \text{递归修正项}$$

$$f_{n+1} - 2f_n + f_{n-1} = m^2 f_n$$
